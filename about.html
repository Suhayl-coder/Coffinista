Our Story

Started off in 2018, I can proudly say that I am the owner of Coffinista coffee shop and the business is doing really well. Since I was a child, I was very fond of the smell of coffee and always wanted to open my own coffee shop. So here I am, officially owning one. I was inspired by coffee shops mainly abroad, the décor, the vibes, the cozy comfy ambiance and wanted to bring that here in Mauritius. I even visited a coffee shop in England which years ago was a public toilet and the people that is the owners made the necessary arrangements and renovations and today it is a well-known coffee shop in England.


I grew this passion of owning a coffee shop ever since I travelled abroad for the first time. More specifically I was inspired by Starbucks because of its franchise all over the world and I made up my mind that I would want to be like that one day. Later, i created my blog on coffee shops where I would go and review coffee shops. I always wanted to make my passion become my daily job so that when I would wake up every morning, I wouldn’t be cursing myself for making a wrong job decision.
